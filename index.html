I find the topic of dieting interesting. Most people have a stigma that you need to eat a strict, tasteless diet to lose weight. While it is a good idea to eat clean foods, you could actually eat nothing but junk food and lose weight if you consume less calories than what's called your maintinance calorie intake. This would be incredibly difficult, however, because you will likely be very hungry since a small amount of junk food tends to have a high caloie content. You'll eat less than if you were to consume the same amount of calories with a healthy diet.
